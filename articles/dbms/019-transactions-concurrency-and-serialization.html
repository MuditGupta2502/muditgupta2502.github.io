Schedules and Correctness

A schedule interleaves operations of multiple transactions. Conflict-serializable schedules preserve correctness equivalent to some serial order.

Serialization Graph

Build precedence graph from read-write/write-read/write-write conflicts. Cycles imply non-serializable schedules.

T1: R(A) W(A) T2: R(A) W(A) If interleaving forms cycle in precedence graph -> not conflict serializable.
