Triggers, Stored Procedures, Recovery Management, and Backups

Reliability | 24 min read

Triggers and Stored Procedures

  • Triggers enforce automatic reactions to INSERT/UPDATE/DELETE.
  • Stored procedures centralize validated business logic near data.
CREATE TRIGGER audit_orders AFTER UPDATE ON Orders FOR EACH ROW INSERT INTO AuditLog(...) VALUES (...);

Recovery Management

Crash recovery relies on write-ahead logging, checkpoints, redo, and undo. Goal: bring database to the latest consistent committed state.

Backup Strategy

  • Full backup for baseline restore.
  • Incremental/differential to reduce backup window.
  • Point-in-time recovery with log replay.
Backups are useful only when restore drills are performed and RTO/RPO targets are met.
